Technology Solutions for Social Good

Economic Enterprises for Humanitarian Development (E2HD), a Washington D.C. based non-profit, employs emerging technologies to address humanitarian issues globally. We focus on projects that impact human security and preserve human rights, the building blocks of a healthy and free society. We aim to enhance freedom through the production of tools and insights which directly impact economic development, business practice, and public policy.
